Bouncing PacMen

Refactored PacMen Exercise

Create as many sprites as you like with the click of a button and watch them bounce around your screen. Includes four types of frogs to generate and surprise whimsical sound effects.

Moving Eyes

Refactored Eyes Exercise

Move your mouse around the screen and notice how Ziltoid the Omniscient, musician Devin Townsend's fictional alien, judges you for not offering him a Starbucks latte. He is, after all, in search of the universe's ultimate cup of coffee.


Bus Tracker

Real-time bus movement

Check the bus routes located between Boston University and MIT in Boston, MA. Implemented using the mapbox JavaScript library and requesting data using the API of the Massachusetts Bay Transportartion Authority.
